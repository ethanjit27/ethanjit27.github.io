Sports

I currently play for the Troy Bruins Varsity hockey team. We are a team based out of Troy, Ohio, not related to the Trojans which is the high school team. We play in the CSSHL hockey league (chshl.club). I have been playing for about a year and a half now and it’s been insanely fun. I’ve put about about 6 points so far in both seasons as last year I was still learning my positions and how to skate to my best ability. I feel a lot better this season and I have confidence I will get more goals. My team also just recently went to the championship game in a Thanksgiving tournament at Notre Dame.

The Troy Bruins are a hockey organization based out of Hobart Arena in Troy, OH. They offer a variety of levels from youth to high school hockey. These levels include, Mites, Squirt, Peewee, Bantam, and HS Leagues. Travel is offered for Squirt and Pewee while Bantam and Mites are house leagues. HS league plays in the CHSHL (Cincinnati High School League).

I don’t know if I will be able to play in college but some of my previous teammates went to Sinclair’s hockey team but they are having trouble getting enough players to form a team but they have a solid program, so I will see if it’s still possible by them time I go there.
