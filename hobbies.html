Hobbies

Hockey

I’ve always been into Hockey since I was a baby, quite literally. My dad has been a die hard penguins fan since he was about 20 when his roommate in college got him into the sport. I’ve witnessed 3 Stanley Cup wins and also seen them go to the playoffs every year since I was born until this past year when they lost their streak. A little over a year ago I decided I would get into the sport as I played soccer when I was younger. Hockey was more fast paced and felt more competitive to me as I’ve watched my whole life and I joined the Troy Bruins club team. I play for varsity on my second year playing and it’s a lot of fun to be able to play a game I’ve watched my whole life.

Photography

Photography - Something I’ve always been interested in is photography. I used to want to do it as a career but that never went through although I have taken it up as a hobby after my dad got me a free camera from his job. It’s an Olympus EM10 Mark II, it’s a mirrorless camera which is a type of digital camera that can take pictures without the mirrored effect, it’s a great camera and I enjoy going out and taking pictures around Dayton especially.
